Comfort • Confidence • Style

Established in 2017, Inner Code is a premier women's undergarment retailer located in Kharghar, Navi Mumbai. We offer a diverse range of lingerie, innerwear, and nightwear designed for everyday comfort and confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide an inclusive and accessible foundation for personal style by offering essential, high-quality basics in a spectrum of skin-tone inclusive and core colors. From perfectly fitted T-shirt, Bandeau, and Plunge bras to everyday undies, comfortable sports bras, supportive shapewear, and thermal layers, this collection is designed to be the versatile base for every wardrobe. We believe that feeling confident and comfortable in your first layer is fundamental, allowing you to build any look you desire."
